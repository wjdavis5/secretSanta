What is Secret Santa?

Secret Santa is a fun way to exchange gifts with a group of people. Each person is assigned another person in the group to give a gift to. The identity of the person you are giving a gift to is kept secret until the gift exchange.

How does this app work?

This app allows you to create a Secret Santa event and invite your friends to participate. Once everyone has joined, you can view the event details and share the event with your friends. Each participant will be able to see who they are giving a gift to, but no one else will be able to see this information.

How do I get started?

Click the login button above to get started. Once you are logged in, you will be able to create a new event. Once you have created an event, you will be able to view the event details and share the event with your friends.
